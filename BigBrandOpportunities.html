How This Works

This page rebuilds itself automatically every morning at 9:00 AM, on Anthropic's servers — not on your Mac or iPhone. It runs whether or not any app, browser tab, or Claude conversation is open on your end. Leaving something open on your device doesn't make it happen faster, and closing everything doesn't stop it.

Install As An App

iPhone: open this page in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen. Mac: open in Safari → Share → Add to Dock. Either way you get a real icon and a full-screen window with no browser bar — the closest thing to a native app without an App Store submission.
